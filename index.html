Celebrating India's AI & DeepTech Innovators

India's deep tech and AI ecosystem is experiencing an unprecedented acceleration. Founders are challenging global incumbents by building sophisticated AI products at a speed and scale that is rapidly putting India on the world map for core technology creation. We are inspired by the audacity and technical depth of these AI-native founders, and we are honored to present the inaugural India AI & DeepTech 100.

Great entrepreneurs are emerging in key talent centers worldwide, but the density of exceptional engineering talent, accelerating research output, and a fast-spreading entrepreneurial mindset makes the Indian ecosystem uniquely positioned. The companies featured in this report are united by more than their technology. They are led by dynamic, resilient founders who are building lean, capital-efficient teams that achieve outsized, global impact.

About This Report

As one of the most active funds in the AI and deep tech space, we receive a high volume of requests regarding where the Indian ecosystem is headed. The intention of this report is to celebrate the companies and early winners emerging out of a vibrant ecosystem of over 1,000+ AI startups.

To create this index, we have profiled 100 of the top VC-funded AI startups operating out of India or built by Indian founders for the world.

Disclaimer: While we have made every effort to be thorough, this list may not be perfectly comprehensive. If you believe we missed a pioneering startup that deserves to be on this list, please reach out to us.
